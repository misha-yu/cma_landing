How to Create a CryptoMeme and Transfer it to Ethereum Blockchain
Additional Info
Creation of a CryptoMeme
1. Blockchain is a technology that let’s create and store unique and protected (with cryptography) digital objects, which you can truly own.
2. Free Blockchain account. When you register in CryptoMeme.art, we create an account for you in Loom blockchain. All operations in Loom blockchain are free!
3. Token. When you create your CryptoMeme, we create your unique digital object in the Loomx blockchain - the crypto token.
4. ‘Fingerprint’ of the art file. When you upload your art file to CryptoMeme.art, we generate a Hash (sort of unique fingerprint of the file) and put it to your crypto token.
5. Crytpomeme. The unique crypto token with the Hash (unique fingerprint of your art file) is a CryptoMeme. It associates with your art original. You own it. And its crypto protected.
Sell CryptoMemes
6. Ethereum. When you create your Cryptomeme we deploy it in Loomx blockchain. But all sale operations take place in Ethereum blockchain. So you need to transfer your CryptoMeme from Loom to Ethereum.
7. Metamask. To use Ethereum you need to install Metamask Chrome application to Chrome Browser. Install Metamask! You can find it here.
8. Add a small amount of money to your Ethereum account. It’s free for DEMO! All operations in Ethereum are paid but cost less than a dollar. You need to add one dollar to Ethereum to provide transfer operation. Later you will get back much more when sell your CryptoMeme. For Demo, we use test blockchain version Rinkeby. You can add it here...
9. Transfer to Ethereum. When you have Metamask Chrome extension and a small amount of money on the Ethereum account you ready to transfer your CryptoMeme to Ethereum. Click the button ‘Set fro Sale’ to start the Transfer process. It will take a few simple steps.
Buy CryptoMemes
10. Buy. It’s not Working for the Demo version yet. Clicking the Button ‘Purchase’ will initiate the process of purchase the CryptoMeme (token) with Metamask. In future we are going to make auctions functionality.
Admire the art
11. Share in Social network. You can share your Cryptomeme in social networks. The system will summarize likes on social networks.
12. Print a sticker. It’s not Working for the Demo version yet. You will be able to print the sticker with your CryptoMeme for a notebook to show that you are the owner of a super cool piece of digital art. Your friends will be thrilled.
